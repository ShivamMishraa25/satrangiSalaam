About the Creator

Shivam Mishra

Hello! I’m Shivam Mishra, the proud creator of this website for All India Satrangi Salaam Association. I am passionate about web development and design, and this project has allowed me to blend my skills with a cause that I truly believe in — supporting LGBTQIA+, human rights, and women’s rights.


Through this project, I have designed and developed every aspect of the site, including the user-friendly interface, responsive design, database integration, and features such as user submissions and moderator approval panels. This work reflects my attention to detail, ability to manage complex web applications, and my passion for creating accessible, inclusive online platforms.


This project has been a meaningful journey, and I am proud to showcase it. I hope this work demonstrates not only my technical skills but also my commitment to causes that matter.


If you like my work and would like to commission me for a similar project, feel free to reach out. I am available for web development projects, specializing in creating dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly websites. You can contact me through email at shivam.m4464@gmail.com or call me at +91 9450101520. I look forward to collaborating on your project and bringing your vision to life.
